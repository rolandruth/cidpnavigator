# 10 — Doctor Visit Prep

## What belongs here

- Standard question lists for neurology appointments
- Questions specific to: newly diagnosed, treatment decision, treatment not working, considering switching treatments
- How to describe neurological symptoms clearly and specifically
- Symptom diary / tracking template
- How to prepare a medication and infusion history summary
- What to bring to appointments: records, infusion logs, symptom notes
- How to discuss treatment costs and access concerns with your neurologist
- Second opinion guidance: when and how to seek one
- Questions to ask about clinical trial eligibility
- Post-appointment: how to follow up on unanswered questions

## Example question sets to include

**Newly diagnosed:**
- What type of CIDP do I have?
- What is my recommended first treatment and why?
- How will we know if the treatment is working?
- What happens if the first treatment doesn't work?

**On treatment:**
- Is my current dosing optimal?
- Should I consider switching to home infusion?
- Are there newer treatment options I should consider?
- What symptoms should prompt me to call before my next appointment?
