## Key phrases for appeal letters

- "medically necessary and not experimental"
- "standard of care per EAN/PNS 2021 clinical practice guideline"
- "FDA-approved indication"
- "failure of [prior treatment] as documented in medical records"
- "delay in treatment will result in irreversible neurological damage"
